## Why Choose SciPhi?

Built on top of the open source R2R framework, SciPhi offers 1-click deployment of RAG solutions, enabling you to focus on building innovative applications. Our cloud platform streamlines the process of bringing your RAG solutions to production, providing a robust and scalable infrastructure.

[Explore the R2R documentation](https://r2r-docs.sciphi.ai/) to learn more about R2R, the engine which powers SciPhi Cloud.
